MODE 03 — JOINT VENTURES

The Hybrid

Shared ownership. Partner brings capital and distribution. We bring build and execution.

The Hybrid is the best of both modes. When a partner has domain expertise, customer relationships, or distribution that would take years to build — and the factory has the build capacity to match — we structure a joint venture with shared ownership and aligned incentives.

No advisory fees. No consulting retainers. Equity alignment from day one. Partners who have skin in the game make better decisions. So do we.
